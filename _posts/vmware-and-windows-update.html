For some reason, Windows Update doesn't seem to work out of the box in VMware. It took me a while to find this so I thought I'll replicate the simple steps I had to take to get it working:
  1. net stop wuauserv
  2. regsvr32 %windir%\system32\wups2.dll
  3. net start wuauserv
