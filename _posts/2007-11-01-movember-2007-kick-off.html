No more shaving starting today. Some office mates (aka mo bros) have cajoled me into taking part in Movember. The idea behind is to grow a moustache (i.e. mo) during the whole month of November to raise awareness and money for fighting prostate cancer.

I'm not sure yet if I'll be posting pictures, but one of our colleagues (aka mo sista) will be taking pictures every 3 days in order to track each mo bro's individual progress.

Oh well, one of these things I'll have to go through, I guess...
